Who we are

About Heart Sync 💡

A fun, personal quiz platform built with love from Ilorin, Nigeria.

What is Heart Sync?

Heart Sync is an interactive personality quiz you create and share with the people closest to you — friends, partners, or family. You answer questions about yourself, share a link, and discover how well they really know you. No app downloads. No sign-ups. Just connection.

How It Works — Step by Step

1

Choose a quiz category — Friendship, Romantic, Relationship, Family, or Other.

2

Enter your name and answer all 15 questions honestly about yourself.

3

Your quiz is encoded into a unique shareable link — no server, no login.

4

Send the link to a friend, partner, or family member via WhatsApp, SMS, or any platform.

5

They open the link, enter their name, and take the quiz in under 2 minutes.

6

Their score is revealed instantly — and added to the friendboard to compare with others!

7

A full answer breakdown shows every question, what they guessed, and what you actually chose.

8

Multiple people can take the same quiz — a leaderboard tracks who knows you best.

Your data

Privacy Policy 🔒

We believe your data is yours. Here's exactly how Heart Sync handles it.

1. No Server, No Storage

Heart Sync does not operate a backend server. Your quiz answers are never sent to or stored on any external server. All data lives in the URL itself, encoded in your shareable link.

2. Local Storage Only

Scores and responses are stored in your browser's localStorage only. Each participant also receives a unique anonymous device ID so their attempts can be tracked separately even if two people share the same name. This data never leaves your device.

3. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of behavioral analytics.

4. Third-Party Images

Quiz option images are loaded from Unsplash (unsplash.com). Unsplash's own privacy policy governs that interaction.

5. AI Chatbot

The AI assistant on our Contact page is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Messages you send to the chatbot are processed by Anthropic. Please do not share sensitive personal information in the chat.
